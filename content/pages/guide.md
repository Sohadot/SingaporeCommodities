# Guides

Guides provide the **foundational layer** of the platform.

They are designed to:
- Clarify concepts
- Establish frameworks
- Enable deeper understanding

## Topics Covered

- What is a commodity node?
- Routing vs production dynamics
- How maritime trade flows operate
- System-level interpretation of markets

## Function

Guides are not simplified explanations.

They are:
> Entry points into structural thinking.

## Audience

Designed for:
- Analysts
- Researchers
- Strategic thinkers
- Curious professionals

## Philosophy

Understanding precedes interpretation.

Interpretation precedes strategy.

## Role in the Platform

Guides act as:
- The base layer
- The conceptual foundation
- The entry into deeper analysis

Without them:
The rest of the platform cannot be fully understood.
