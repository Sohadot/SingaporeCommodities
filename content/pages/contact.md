# Contact

SingaporeCommodities.com is not a mass-interaction platform.

Communication is **selective, intentional, and purpose-driven**.

## Inquiries Accepted

We consider messages related to:
- Strategic partnerships
- Data or research collaboration
- Institutional interest
- Acquisition discussions

## Not Accepted

We do not respond to:
- Generic outreach
- Marketing solicitations
- Irrelevant proposals

## Contact Channel

Email:
agent@sohadot.com

## Response Policy

Due to the nature of the platform:
- Not all messages receive a response
- Priority is given to high-signal communication

## Positioning

This platform is built as a **long-term digital asset**.

Communication reflects that standard.
