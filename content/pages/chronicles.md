# Chronicles

Chronicles document **events that reshape systems**.

Not news.  
Not headlines.  
But structural shifts.

## What is a Chronicle?

A chronicle captures:
- A disruption
- A realignment
- A pressure point
- A transition in flow dynamics

## Examples

- Energy route disruptions
- Policy shifts affecting trade corridors
- Strategic repositioning of regions
- Infrastructure-level changes

## Approach

Each chronicle is:
- Contextualized
- Interpreted structurally
- Linked to broader system implications

## Why It Matters

Markets react to events.

But systems are **reshaped by them**.

Chronicles focus on:
> The shape, not the noise.

## Positioning

This section transforms isolated events into:
- Patterns
- Signals
- Strategic indicators
