## Related Concepts

This framework operates alongside a set of closely linked concepts:

- [Routing Power](/framework/routing-power/) — the ability to shape global flows by structuring their pathways
- Predictability Dominance — sustained influence derived from reliability rather than coercion
- Systemic Trade Control — indirect control over trade through conditions of movement rather than ownership
- Discipline Under Constraint — the emergence of power through tightly managed limitations
- Trusted Passage — the establishment of routes that global actors rely on without hesitation
